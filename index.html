How we met

Farrah and I first met during our ICST201: Data Structures class in University during the 2nd semester of 2012. We first started talking when she got dismissed from being the class beadle on the account of her being late (every single time). Years later, we would meet again in another class, Moral Theology where I grew close with her and her clique. I found myself liking her more and more until I finally got the courage to ask her out on a date a few weeks after our graduation. I thought it would all be butterflies and roses when she said yes, but fortune doesn't always favor the bold. After getting rejected by her FIVE times, I thought that was it, but we remained friends and hung out until suddenly everything just clicked. The rest is history <3.

Very yes! There is enough parking space for everyone and it's FREE!

7. I said YES sa RSVP, kaya lang di na pala ako makaka-attend. What should I do?

PLEASE, huwag po mahihiya na magsabi kung sakaling may emergency or may conflict sa inyong schedule. Of course this will be sad on our part, but please do know that we fully understand. We just request that you inform us ahead of time so we can organize our guest list accordingly.

8. I said NO sa RSVP, pero makaka-attend na pala ako. What should I do?

Let us know asap. However, please understand that we took your "NO" as a confirmation. We may already have reallocated your seat/s to others, therefore we cannot guarantee the same number of seats or one at all.

Nevertheless, we'll check any last-minute cancellations, but please do not be offended if we are unable to accommodate you.

9. Pwede ba mag-plus one? (Date, juwa, partner, sibling, kids, driver, yaya, etc.)

Please refer to your invitations for the number of seat/s allocated for you.

As much as we want to invite everyone to celebrate our union, unfortunately, we can only accommodate a very limited number of guests due to the venue's capacity, budget, and of course safety restrictions.

Be reminded that we will give our coordinators the right to refuse entry to those who are not on our guest list.

SERIOUSLY. DON'T DO THIS PLEASE! Silence OR NO reaction means NO.

We will get in touch with your from time to time to confirm your attendance.

Pag wala po talagang sagot before the RSVP deadline, sad to say but we can no longer put you on our guest list.

Again, our coordinators have the right to refuse entry to those who are not on our guest list.

11. May safety guidelines ba?

At this point, Philippines doesn't implement stringent COVID-19 restrictions. But for us, we want you to be safe as well as others:

  •   Wear your mask properly
  •   Clean your hands frequently with alcohol-based hand run or sanitizer
  •   Refrain from transferring seats
  •   Cover your mouth and nose with a bent elbow or tissue when you cough or sneeze. Dispose used tissues immediately and clean hands regularly
  •   If you develop symptoms or test positive for COVID-19, please send us a message, stay at home, rest & recuperate
